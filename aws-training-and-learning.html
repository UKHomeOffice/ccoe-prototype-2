AWS OGVA training

AWS reserves the right to remove any attendee that is not a member of an OGVA organisation. Attendees can create an AWS account and by clicking the ‘Create Your Amazon Account’ option. Please see the ‘OGVA Registration’ page for further details and a step-by-step guide.

We kindly request you note:

  • All attendees must have a training account to be able to register for the OGVA training schedule.
  • If an attendee can no longer attend, they must withdraw their registration as there may be other people on the waitlist.
  • Attendees must register at least 14 days in advance, to receive the course confirmation and training material.

AWS public training

As well as the AWS Embrace training platform for government users, they have their own AWS training platform that includes e-learning and AWS skill builder.

When registering for an AWS account please:

  • Use your professional email address.
  • If you have an existing AWS account, please update your profile to include your employer details.

All classroom courses and old transcripts will remain on AWS training.

AWS skill builder

All self-paced digital courses are now on AWS Training and Certification’s new learning centre, AWS Skill Builder.

AWS game days

We can organise AWS GameDays for you and your team as and when required. If you are interested in arranging a cloud game day, please contact: public.cloud@homeoffice.gov.uk
