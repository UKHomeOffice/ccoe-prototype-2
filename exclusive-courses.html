Upcoming AWS courses

Contents

View the AWS training calendar for sessions delivered by AWS technical trainers. To sign up for one of the exclusive courses you must have an aws training account registered with your Home Office email address. You can create a free AWS account by registering here.

AWS training calendar

The following listed courses have been provided by AWS and you can find more information by clicking on the links. This calendar will update as and when more courses are made available by AWS. Check back periodically for updates.

Please note all these courses are exclusive for government employees so you should sign in to your Home Office AWS training account.
