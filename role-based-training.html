AWS Training

Role based training

On the job family pages (Data, IT operations, Product and delivery, QAT, UCD) you can find useful training links based on your job role. The training links include:

  • Learning paths for a high-level view of relevant AWS training
  • Ramp-up guides for step-by-step guides for cloud training and provides links to various resources

The section below shows all of the DDaT job roles split between DDaT job families. Each job family has a page where you can find recommended training specific to your job role.
