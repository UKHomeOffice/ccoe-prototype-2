Amazon Web Services (AWS) Identity and Access Management (IAM) account requests

New user next steps

If you are a new user, you will receive your credentials with a detailed email informing you what to do next. The contents of the email are as followed:

To successfully use your account there are several required steps that we ask you to take before using your account:

  1. Read all of section 1 before taking any action.
  2. Read all of section 2 before or after setting up.
  3. Read section 3 if you require assistance switching roles.
  4. Read section 4 if you require any further assistance or would like to know what free training is available.

Section 1

Please find attached your credentials for logging into AWS, your account is set up to change password when you log in and you will need to setup your Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) before you can assume any roles.

Before you try to switch roles, please read the following; once you have MFA set up make sure you log out of AWS by signing out, close your current browser window, and open a new one to log back in. If you don’t your current AWS session will not acknowledge the MFA set up properly, and not allow you to switch roles.

Section 2 - Important!

You must:

  • Set up a new password on first log in - the password must be at least 9 characters long consisting of; 1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number, 1 special character - (! @ # $ % ^ & * ( ) _ + - = [ ] { } | ').
  • Set up MFA
  • Remember to update your password every 90 days
  • If you set up an Access Key, you must rotate it every 90 days
  • You must remember to log in periodically to at least do the above, so we know your account is still active.
  • Reports are run periodically to check the above, and if you are not compliant your account could be suspended.

Please note when you log-in you are accessing the central log-in account. You have very limited permissions in this account. You will need to switch roles into your assigned role within your area's accounts.

Back to side navigation

Section 3 - steps to switch roles

  • Navigate to the top right of the AWS console and click on your email address (this will generate a dropdown window)
  • Click on 'Switch Roles' (this will direct you to a page - titled 'Switch Role')
  • Add your account number, role name and choose a display name (this can be whatever you want) - optionally you can also change the colour.

More information on how to switch roles via the console can be found on the AWS site.

If you would prefer to know how to switch roles switch roles via the AWS CLI, this is available via the AWS site. via the console can be found on the AWS Amazon site.

Section 4

For further assistance please see the rest of the Cloud Centre of Excellence (CCoE), which has cloud guidance and tips available as well as details of cloud training. You can also get in touch with the team through our mailbox or on slack.
