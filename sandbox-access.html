The Cloud Vending Team having ownership of the CTI: Tooling Sandpit which is the HO Central Sandpit Area. This area has been used by number of teams to test deployments and methodologies before they touch Pre-Prod or Production accounts.

The other sandboxes/sandpits are managed by the teams who own them, and they are all in use for testing and training purposes.

How to get access to sandbox accounts

To get access to the above sandbox accounts please follow our IAM ticket raising process and include the sandbox details you want access to. Please note the CTI Tooling Sandpit does not require approval when making an IAM request but all other sandboxes require approval as normal.
